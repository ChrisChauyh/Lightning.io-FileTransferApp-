Welcome to Lightning.io

This is a website that for temporary upload and download files

It is a super simple file sharing service that lets you upload files or text and get a link or QR code to share. The files can be automatically deleted after they are downloaded or expired. You can also review your uploaded files by loging in and check out other user's files or texts
